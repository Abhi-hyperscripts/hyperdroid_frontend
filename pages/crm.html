Features

Everything you need to manage your sales pipeline

From lead capture to deal close — a complete CRM built for teams that want visibility, speed, and zero missed opportunities.

Visual Deal Pipeline

Drag deals through customizable stages. Multiple pipelines, win probability tracking, stage history with time-in-stage analytics.

Lead Capture & Webhooks

Receive leads from any source via webhooks. Custom field mapping, auto-assignment, quality scoring. Redis-backed async processing with retry.

Facebook Lead Ads

Native OAuth integration with Facebook. Auto-import leads from Lead Ad forms. Real-time webhook notifications. Multi-page support.

Real-Time Notifications

SignalR-powered live updates. 16 event types: new leads, deal stage changes, task assignments, won/lost deals. Push notifications to mobile.

Contact & Company Management

Full contact database linked to companies and deals. Custom fields, tags, activity history. Track every interaction across your pipeline.

Task & Activity Tracking

Log calls, emails, meetings, notes. Assign tasks with priorities and due dates. Track overdue items. Full activity timeline per entity.
